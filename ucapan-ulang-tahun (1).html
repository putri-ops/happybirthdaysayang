A Letter, Just For You

To The One I Love

I've been sitting here trying to find the right words, but nothing feels big enough to hold everything I feel about you.

Thank you for the quiet mornings, the ridiculous jokes, the way you make hard days feel lighter just by being there. You've become the calmest, warmest part of my life.

Today is about celebrating you — not just who you are, but everything you mean to me. I hope this year brings you everything you deserve, and I hope I get to be there for all of it.

Happy birthday, my love. Here's to us, and to everything still ahead.

Always yours
